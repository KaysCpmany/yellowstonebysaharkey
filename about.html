Our Mission: Connecting People with Nature

At EverWild Expeditions, we believe that nature has the power to transform lives. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between people and the natural world through carefully crafted adventures that inspire awe, foster understanding, and create lifelong memories.

Founded in 1998 by park ranger Michael Thompson, we've grown from a single guide operation to one of the most respected adventure companies in the American West, without ever losing sight of our core values: conservation, education, and authentic experiences.

Michael Thompson
Founder & Chief Explorer

Our Journey Through Time

1998

Humble Beginnings

Michael Thompson starts EverWild Expeditions with just one van and a passion for sharing the park's wonders. Our first tour had only 6 guests!

2005

Conservation Partnership

We establish our Conservation Fund, donating 5% of all profits to park preservation and wildlife protection initiatives.

2012

National Recognition

Received the "Eco-Tourism Operator of the Year" award for our sustainable practices and educational programs.

2020

Expanding Horizons

Launched our Grand Teton and Rocky Mountain expeditions, expanding beyond Yellowstone while maintaining our commitment to quality.
